                    <h3>Malware</h3>
                    <p>
                        Logiciels malveillants conçus pour endommager, perturber ou obtenir un accès 
                        non autorisé à un système informatique.
                    </p>
                    <div class="threat-types">
                        <span class="threat-badge">Virus</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Vers</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Trojans</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Spyware</span>
                    </div>

                        <span>Sévérité: Élevée</span>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="threat-card" data-threat="phishing">
                    <div class="threat-icon">
                        <i class="fas fa-fish"></i>
                    </div>
                    <h3>Phishing</h3>
                    <p>
                        Tentatives frauduleuses d'obtenir des informations sensibles en se faisant 
                        passer pour une entité de confiance.
                    </p>
                    <div class="threat-types">
                        <span class="threat-badge">Email Phishing</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Spear Phishing</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Whaling</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Smishing</span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="threat-severity critical">
                        <span>Sévérité: Critique</span>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="threat-card" data-threat="ransomware">
                    <div class="threat-icon">
                        <i class="fas fa-lock"></i>
                    </div>
                    <h3>Ransomware</h3>
                    <p>
                        Malware qui chiffre les données de la victime et exige une rançon pour 
                        fournir la clé de déchiffrement.
                    </p>
                    <div class="threat-types">
                        <span class="threat-badge">Crypto Ransomware</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Locker Ransomware</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Double Extortion</span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="threat-severity critical">
                        <span>Sévérité: Critique</span>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="threat-card" data-threat="ddos">
                    <div class="threat-icon">
                        <i class="fas fa-server"></i>
                    </div>
                    <h3>Attaques DDoS</h3>
                    <p>
                        Attaques visant à rendre un service indisponible en le submergeant de 
                        trafic malveillant.
                    </p>
                    <div class="threat-types">
                        <span class="threat-badge">Volume-Based</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Protocol Attacks</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Application Layer</span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="threat-severity high">
                        <span>Sévérité: Élevée</span>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="threat-card" data-threat="mitm">
                    <div class="threat-icon">
                        <i class="fas fa-exchange-alt"></i>
                    </div>
                    <h3>Man-in-the-Middle</h3>
                    <p>
                        Interception des communications entre deux parties pour espionner ou 
                        altérer les données échangées.
                    </p>
                    <div class="threat-types">
                        <span class="threat-badge">Session Hijacking</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">SSL Stripping</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">DNS Spoofing</span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="threat-severity high">
                        <span>Sévérité: Élevée</span>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="threat-card" data-threat="sql">
                    <div class="threat-icon">
                        <i class="fas fa-database"></i>
                    </div>
                    <h3>Injection SQL</h3>
                    <p>
                        Insertion de code SQL malveillant dans les requêtes pour manipuler ou 
                        voler des données de la base.
                    </p>
                    <div class="threat-types">
                        <span class="threat-badge">In-band SQLi</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Blind SQLi</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Out-of-band SQLi</span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="threat-severity high">
                        <span>Sévérité: Élevée</span>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="threat-card" data-threat="xss">
                    <div class="threat-icon">
                        <i class="fas fa-code"></i>
                    </div>
                    <h3>Cross-Site Scripting (XSS)</h3>
                    <p>
                        Injection de scripts malveillants dans des pages web vues par d'autres 
                        utilisateurs.
                    </p>
                    <div class="threat-types">
                        <span class="threat-badge">Stored XSS</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">Reflected XSS</span>
                        <span class="threat-badge">DOM-based XSS</span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="threat-severity medium">
                        <span>Sévérité: Moyenne</span>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="threat-card" data-threat="social">
                    <div class="threat-icon">
                        <i class="fas fa-users"></i>
                    </div>
                    <h3>Ingénierie Sociale</h3>
                    <p>

                        confidentielles ou un accès non autorisé.
                    </p>
                    <div class="threat-types">
                        <span class="threat-badge">Pretexting</span>
